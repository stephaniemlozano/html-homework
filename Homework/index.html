Hiking the Blue Ridge Mountains

Keep reading for information about easy to moderate trails to hike (with a toddler!) in or around the Blue Ridge Mountains.

When hiking any trail you want to make sure you have the essentials.

  • Appropriate clothing and footwear
  • Food
  • Water
  • First aid kit
  • Map and compass
  • Knife or multi-tool
  • A hiking backpack to hold your supplies
  • If you plan on bringing your toddler a carrier is a must!

*Safety First! You should never hike alone BUT if you do, make sure to let someone know where you are hiking and when you are scheduled to return.*
