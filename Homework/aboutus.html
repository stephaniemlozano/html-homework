Our favorite place to pick berries is Starkey Market & Farms.

They have 3 different types of berries to pick from: Strawberries, Blueberries and Blackberries.



We highly recommend visiting Starkey Market if you are in Pasco County!

They also have a farm area with cows and a barn. Local produce is sold in the barn.
